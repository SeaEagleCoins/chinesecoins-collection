Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of 6 Dynasties coins

In the South, the Chinese dominance were made up of the following dynasties:
Liu Song AD 420 - 479
Southern Chi AD 479 - 502
Liang AD 502 - 557: "Gong Shi Nu Qian" wu zhu and Tai Qing Feng Le coins were issued.
Chen AD 557 - 589

In the North, the non-Han Chinese dynasties were:
Northern Wei AD 386 - 535: "Yong An Wu Zhu" and "Tai He Wu Zhu" coins were issued.
Eastern Wei AD 534 - 549
Western Wei AD 535 - 557:
Northern Qi AD 549 - 577: "Chang Ping Wu Zhu" coins were issued.
Nothern Zhou AD 557 - 581: "Bu Quan", "Yong Tong Wan Guo" and "Wu Xing Da Bu".
