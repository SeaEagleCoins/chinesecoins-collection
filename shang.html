Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of Shang Dynasty Money

The Shang Dynasty is considered as China's earliest dynasty by many historians. This is evidenced by the discoveries of major royal tombs and foundations of palaces in AnYang containing huge caches of weapons, bronzewares, shells, jade, bone, and stone artifacts. The predoiminant system of currencies was cowrie. As natural cowrie shells were scarce in China. Cowrie imitiations in clay, stone, bone, jade, and bronze were also made for general circulation. The Shang was one of the earliest civilization in the world to use bronze as money.
