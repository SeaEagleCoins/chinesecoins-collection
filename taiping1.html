Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of TaiPing Rebellion coins

The TaiPing Rebellion was a civil war in the late imperial China between the Qing dynasty and the rebel TaiPing Heavenly Kingdom. The war lasted 14 years from 1850 to 1871. Most of the coins issued by the rebels were 1 cash coins and have the same inscriptions; "Tai Ping Tian Guo" - (Heavenly Kingdom) on the obverse and " Sheng Bao" - (Holy Treasure) on the reverse. The large denomination; 10 cash and 50 cash TaiPing Tian Guo coins are very rare.
