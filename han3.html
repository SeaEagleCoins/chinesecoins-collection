Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of Han coins

The 8 zhu Ban Liang was introduced in 186BC during the reign of Empress Lu Zhi of the Western Han Dynasty. Empress Lu Zhi was the empress consort of GaoZu, the founder of the Han Dynasty, and became the first woman to assume the tiltle of Empress of China after GaoZu's death. The diameter of these coins are about 30mm with a weight of about 6gm or more.
