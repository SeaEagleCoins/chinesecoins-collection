Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of Zhou Dynasty Knife Money

During the Zhou Dynasty money in the shape of actual knife were used as a medium of exchange. Broadly these knife money can classified into the following types based on its shape: (i) the big heavy knife money, the Qi Dao, (ii) needle tip knife money, (iii) pointed tip knife money, (iv) straight knife money, and (v) the Ming knife money which takes its name from the character on its obverse. The Ming knife money are the most common among all these knife money.
