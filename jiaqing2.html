Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of JiaQing coins

The JiaQing Tong Bao was the official cash coin issued during the reign of Emperor Jia Qing. Like its predecessor, only 1 cash coins were issued. JiaQing coins were generally well made and of good quality. The reverse of the coin has Manchu cahracters indicating the name or location of the mint. Some Jiaqing coins features dots, representing "star", or crescent representing "moon" on the reverse.
