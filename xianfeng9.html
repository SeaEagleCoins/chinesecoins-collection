Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of XianFeng coins

The Xian Feng 100 cash are large with diameter ranging between 48mm to 62mm. These coins are highly sought after by collectors and are often referred to as multiple cash coins. Like most Qing Dynasty coins, they are made of brass and feature Manchu and Chinese scripts. The large multiple cash coins of 50 and 100 cash were considered overvalued in proportion to the amount of metal used to cast them. A relatively large number of multiple cash coins were minted during the Xian Feng reign as compared to other periods during the Qing Dynasty. These large denomination XianFeng Yuan Bao coins were issued mainly to finance the war during the TaiPing Rebellion. This was a turbulent period coupled with severe inflation.
