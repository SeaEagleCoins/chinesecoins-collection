Ancient Chinese coins: A brief history of Shang Dynasty Fish Money

Before standard circular or round coins were widely minted, ancient Chinese fashioned money in the forms of tools implements like farming spades, weapons like knives. ornaments like bells as well as cowrie and fish. These forms of currencies are usually cast in bronze as bronze wes considered as a precious metal which can be traded.

The Fish Money in China primarily refers to the ancient bronze fish money (Yu Bi). To the Chinese, fish is a cultural symbolism representing wealth and surplus.
